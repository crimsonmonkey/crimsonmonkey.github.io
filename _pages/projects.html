Lab Members

 

Ksenia V Krasileva

Group Leader (from July 2018 Assistant Professor at University of California Berkeley). A scientist interested in Genomics and Plant Immunity.

Erin Baggs

PhD student (2nd year), my research focuses on using an evolutionary perspective to explore NLR plant disease resistance gene diversity across diverse plant populations and species. Comparative genomic predictions we make upon studying NLR evolution are then functionally validated in model lab systems such as Nicotiana and Arabidopsis. 

Andrew Deatker

Academic Specialist for the Krasileva lab, involved in plant pathogen interactions between Wheat and the disease Stripe Rust. Phenotyper of Field trials, Orderer of the Stores, Keeper of the Seeds, Flyer of Drones and an all round amazing person (and humble too)
