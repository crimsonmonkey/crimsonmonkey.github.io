Code

Plant R-genes

Scripts to annotate Pfam domains and extract NLR plant immune receptors and their architectures as published in Sarris et al BMC Biology 2016.

SCVEP

Annotation-free variant effect prediction.

Wheat TILLING PNAS 2017

Collaborative set of scripts used in Krasileva et al 2017 Wheat TILLING publication.

Tandem

Identification of neighboring genes, used in Bailey et al. 2017.

TGACv1-circos

Scripts used to plot genetic map of wheat used in Clavijo et al 2017.

LRR crawler

Extracting leucine Rich Repeat Motifs with regular expression.
