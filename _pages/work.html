Code 

Our code is maintained at GitHub. https://github.com/krasileva-group/

Available under MIT license.

Plant R-genes

https://github.com/krasileva-group/plant_rgenes

Scripts to annotate Pfam domains and extract NLR plant immune receptors and their architectures as published in Sarris et al BMC Biology 2016.

SCVEP

https://github.com/krasileva-group/scvep

Annotation-free variant effect prediction. It is based on blasting DNA sequence surrounding the SNP against protein database and resulting alignment is used to predict likely effect of the SNP on the coding sequence.

Wheat TILLING PNAS 2017

https://github.com/krasileva-group/wheat_tilling_pub

Collaborative set of scripts used in Krasileva et al 2017 Wheat TILLING publication. Forked from repository.

Tandem

https://github.com/krasileva-group/tandem

Identification of neighboring genes, used in Bailey et al. 2017.

TGACv1-circos

https://github.com/krasileva-group/tgacv1-circos

Scripts used to plot genetic map of wheat used in Clavijo et al. 2017. See also ordering wheat genome scaffolds using genetic maps.

LRR crawler

https://github.com/krasileva-group/lrr_crawler

Extracting leucine Rich Repeat Motifs with regular expression. Currently, under development.
